# Bonus
### Transworld PeopleOps Portal — a guide for the team

This is part of the portal guide. It's written to be read alongside the app —
press the **?** on any page for the same help in a panel. The portal is a
**control room**: it records and checks pay, bonus and compliance evidence;
HumanManager and Remita still pay people. Figures shown in the portal are
provisional records in Naira (₦).

---
### Bonus

The bonus control room. It builds the annual bonus round from the audited result — the pool (15% of PBT), each person's target months by grade, and their scorecard multiplier — for RemCo to approve. The portal computes and records; it does not pay.

**Who can use this:** Finance manages (bonus.manage); Exec (RemCo) approves (bonus.approve).

**What you can do here:**

- **Open a round** — Starts a bonus round.
- **Open a round to work it** — Goes to the round's worksheet.

**Where this sits:** Bonus is paid in April against the prior year's audited financials (published 30 March). Pool → target months → multiplier → confirm → RemCo approves → lock. Deferrals (Phase B) carry on the deferral ledger.

**Good to know:**

- Pool = 0.15 × PBT; individual = target months × monthly gross × multiplier (×0–1.3); the integrity gate can zero it.
- Figures are provisional control records — payment runs elsewhere.

---

### Bonus deferrals (Phase B)

The deferral ledger for the part of senior bonuses held back and paid later — with clawback and leaver-forfeiture. This is the one place a sealed bonus figure can still move, by design.

**Who can use this:** Finance manages payment/settlement (bonus.manage); RemCo handles clawback/forfeit/reinstate (bonus.approve).

**What you can do here:**

- **Mark paid / settle year** — Records a deferred tranche as paid, or settles a year's deferrals.
- **Clawback / forfeit leaver / reinstate** — RemCo claws back, forfeits a leaver's deferral, or reinstates one.

**Good to know:**

- Phase B is the deliberate exception to "locked = immutable" — handled here, with audit.
- Clawback and forfeiture sit with the approval tier, not the preparer.

---

### Bonus round

One bonus round's worksheet: the inputs (PBT, pool), each person's target months and multiplier, the per-row confirmation, and the approve-and-lock. Prepared by Finance, approved by RemCo.

**Who can use this:** Finance prepares; RemCo approves & locks.

**What you can do here:**

- **Set inputs / multiplier** — Enters the PBT/pool and each person's multiplier (from calibration).
- **Confirm / reopen a row** — Confirms a person's figure, or reopens it to amend.
- **Submit for approval** — Sends the round to RemCo; it locks for editing.
- **Approve** — RemCo approves — a different person from the preparer. (_two people, on purpose_)
- **Lock** — Seals the round as permanent evidence. (_permanent once done_)

**Good to know:**

- Preparer ≠ approver; a locked round is permanent.
- G4/G5 awards split per the model; deferral portions move to the Phase B ledger.
