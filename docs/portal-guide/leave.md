# Leave
### Transworld PeopleOps Portal — a guide for the team

This is part of the portal guide. It's written to be read alongside the app —
press the **?** on any page for the same help in a panel. The portal is a
**control room**: it records and checks pay, bonus and compliance evidence;
HumanManager and Remita still pay people. Figures shown in the portal are
provisional records in Naira (₦).

---
### Leave

The leave hub: your own balances and requests, plus — for managers and People Ops — the requests waiting on you. Annual, sick and other leave types all flow through here.

**Who can use this:** Everyone requests leave; managers recommend; People Ops decides (leave.manage).

**What you can do here:**

- **Request leave** — Opens the request form for yourself.
- **Open a request** — Opens a request to review or act on it.

**Where this sits:** Employee requests → manager recommends → People Ops approves or declines → balance updates.

**Good to know:**

- Your balance only moves once a request is approved.
- Managers recommend; the formal decision sits with People Ops.

---

### Leave balances

Everyone's entitlements and remaining balances by leave type. People Ops sets and adjusts the entitlements that the request flow draws down.

**Who can use this:** Viewable for your own; People Ops manages entitlements (leave.manage).

**What you can do here:**

- **Save entitlement** — Sets or adjusts a person's entitlement for a leave type (audited).

**Good to know:**

- Entitlements set here are the ceiling the request flow checks against.

---

### Leave request

One leave request in detail — its dates, type, status, and the manager and People-Ops decisions on it.

**Who can use this:** The requester (view), the manager (recommend), People Ops (decide).

**What you can do here:**

- **Recommend / decline (manager)** — A line manager records a recommendation on a report's request. Available to the request's manager.
- **Approve / decline** — People Ops makes the binding decision; an approval updates the balance.
- **Cancel / edit** — The requester can cancel; People Ops can edit a request with a note.

**Good to know:**

- Once approved, the balance has moved — cancelling restores it, with an audit trail.

---

### Leave types

The catalogue of leave types the firm offers (annual, sick, compassionate, and so on) and their rules.

**Who can use this:** People Ops (leave.manage).

**What you can do here:**

- **Save leave type** — Adds or edits a leave type and its settings.

**Good to know:**

- Changing a type's rules affects future requests, not ones already decided.

---

### Request leave

Where you book time off — pick the leave type, the dates, and add a note. The request goes to your manager and then People Ops.

**Who can use this:** Every employee, for themselves.

**What you can do here:**

- **Submit request** — Sends the request for review. The system always books it against you, the signed-in user — not whoever a form names.

**Where this sits:** The first step of the leave flow; track it on the request page once submitted.

**Good to know:**

- Check your balance first — over-booking will be caught at decision, not here.
