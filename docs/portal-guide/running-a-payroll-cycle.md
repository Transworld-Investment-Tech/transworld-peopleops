# Running a payroll cycle
### Transworld PeopleOps Portal — a guide for the team

This is part of the portal guide. It's written to be read alongside the app —
press the **?** on any page for the same help in a panel. The portal is a
**control room**: it records and checks pay, bonus and compliance evidence;
HumanManager and Remita still pay people. Figures shown in the portal are
provisional records in Naira (₦).

---
### Payroll Run

The monthly payroll control sheet — a clean, checked copy of the month's pay that you review, get approved, export and lock as the record. It does not pay anyone: HumanManager and Remita still calculate and pay. This is your monthly "double-check and file".

**Who can use this:** Finance prepares (payroll.manage); an executive approves (payroll.approve).

**What you can do here:**

- **Open a cycle** — Opens the month — pulls in active staff and works out deductions. Only one cycle open at a time.
- **Open a cycle to work it** — Goes to the month's control sheet.

**Where this sits:** Open → review each row → adjust → confirm all → submit → executive approves → export Excel → lock. See the "Running a payroll cycle" guide.

**Good to know:**

- The portal is a cross-check, not the payer — if it disagrees with HumanManager, stop and find out why.
- Only one cycle open at a time; a locked month is permanent.

---

### Payroll cycle

One month's payroll in detail: a row per employee (basic, utility, gross, pension, NHF, ITF, PAYE, net), running totals, and the prepare→approve→lock controls. This is the page the payroll guide walks through.

**Who can use this:** Finance prepares; an executive approves & locks.

**What you can do here:**

- **Adjust a row** — Edits a person's pay for the month (raise, unpaid leave, one-off) with a note, and confirms it.
- **Confirm / reopen a row** — Confirms a correct row, or reopens one to look again. All rows must be confirmed to submit.
- **Set 13th month** — Applies the 13th-month treatment to the cycle where due.
- **Submit for approval** — Sends the cycle to an executive; it locks for editing.
- **Approve** — An executive approves the run — a different person from the preparer. (_two people, on purpose_)
- **Lock as evidence** — Seals the month as the permanent, read-only record (Excel still exportable). (_permanent once done_)

**Good to know:**

- Preparer ≠ approver; the same person never does both.
- Deductions: pension 8%×basic, NHF 2.5%×basic, ITF 1%×basic (in net from Apr 2026), PAYE on the active ruleset.
- Always write a note when you change a row — for future-you and the auditor.
