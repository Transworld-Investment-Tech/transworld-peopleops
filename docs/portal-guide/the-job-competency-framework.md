# The job & competency framework
### Transworld PeopleOps Portal — a guide for the team

This is part of the portal guide. It's written to be read alongside the app —
press the **?** on any page for the same help in a panel. The portal is a
**control room**: it records and checks pay, bonus and compliance evidence;
HumanManager and Remita still pay people. Figures shown in the portal are
provisional records in Naira (₦).

---
### Job & Competency

The library of job profiles and the competency framework behind them: each role's grade, family and track, its job description, and the competencies and scorecard that define what "good" looks like. This is the firm's definition of every role.

**Who can use this:** Viewable by most; People Ops maintains it (jobframework.manage).

**What you can do here:**

- **Open a profile** — Opens a role's full definition.
- **New job profile** — Creates a new role definition.
- **Competencies** — Opens the competency library (the 22 competencies in 7 categories).

**Where this sits:** Feeds compensation (grade → band) and performance (scorecard → bonus multiplier). Set the right grade, family, track and scorecard here and the reward maths follow.

**Good to know:**

- Grade lives on the profile; a person can override it on their own record. Changing a profile's grade moves everyone on it.
- Set family / track / rung / control-function flag on any new role.

---

### Competency library

The master list of the firm's 22 competencies across 7 categories, each rated at Foundational, Proficient or Expert. Scorecards are built by selecting and weighting from this library.

**Who can use this:** Viewable by most; editable by People Ops.

**What you can do here:**

- **New competency** — Adds a competency to the library.
- **Edit competency** — Updates a competency's definition or levels.

**Good to know:**

- This is the shared vocabulary — edits ripple into every scorecard that uses the competency.

---

### Job profile

One role's full definition — grade, family, track, the job description, the competency set and the scorecard weights that drive appraisal and bonus.

**Who can use this:** Viewable by most; editable by People Ops (jobframework.manage).

**What you can do here:**

- **Edit profile** — Changes grade, family, track and details for the role.
- **Edit scorecard** — Sets the competency weights (40–60 / 20–30 / 20–30) that the bonus multiplier reads.

**Good to know:**

- The scorecard weights here decide the indicative score; the leadership family carries its own 55/20/25 weighting.

---

### New job profile

Creates a new role: its grade, family, track and description. Build its scorecard afterwards.

**Who can use this:** People Ops (jobframework.manage).

**What you can do here:**

- **Create profile** — Adds the role to the library.

**Good to know:**

- Set family, track, rung and the control-function flag now — they drive reward and the CCO sign-off rule in hiring.

---

### Edit job profile

The form to change a role's grade, family, track and description.

**Who can use this:** People Ops (jobframework.manage).

**What you can do here:**

- **Save profile** — Writes the role definition (audited).

**Good to know:**

- Changing the grade re-positions everyone holding this profile who hasn't set a personal grade.

---

### New competency

Adds a new competency to the firm-wide library.

**Who can use this:** People Ops (jobframework.manage).

**What you can do here:**

- **Create competency** — Adds it to the 7-category framework.

**Good to know:**

- Place it in the right category — categories carry the family weighting.

---

### Edit competency

The form to revise a single competency's name, category and level descriptors.

**Who can use this:** People Ops (jobframework.manage).

**What you can do here:**

- **Save competency** — Writes the competency (audited).

**Good to know:**

- Changing a competency affects every scorecard referencing it.

---

### Edit scorecard

Sets which competencies count for this role and how they're weighted. The scorecard is what the appraisal scores against and what the bonus multiplier is computed from.

**Who can use this:** People Ops (jobframework.manage).

**What you can do here:**

- **Save scorecard** — Stores the competency weights for the role.
- **Delete scorecard** — Removes the scorecard (the role falls back to defaults).

**Good to know:**

- Per-scorecard category split must stay inside 40–60 / 20–30 / 20–30; the integrity gate can still zero a multiplier regardless of score.
