# Your self-service
### Transworld PeopleOps Portal — a guide for the team

This is part of the portal guide. It's written to be read alongside the app —
press the **?** on any page for the same help in a panel. The portal is a
**control room**: it records and checks pay, bonus and compliance evidence;
HumanManager and Remita still pay people. Figures shown in the portal are
provisional records in Naira (₦).

---
### My Bonus

Your own bonus position — your award from approved rounds and any deferred portion. Read-only.

**Who can use this:** Every employee, for themselves.

**What you can do here:**

- **View my bonus** — Shows your bonus from approved/locked rounds and any Phase B deferral.

**Good to know:**

- Bonus is paid in April against audited results; figures here are the recorded award, not a payment confirmation.

---

### My Documents

Your personal document shelf: letters and forms the firm has issued to you, anything awaiting your signature, and a place to upload your own copies (IDs, certificates).

**Who can use this:** Every employee, for their own documents.

**What you can do here:**

- **Sign a document** — Records your acknowledgement / signature on a document issued to you.
- **Upload a document** — Adds your own file (e.g. a certificate) to your shelf.

**Where this sits:** Documents People Ops generates for you land here for signature; signed copies feed your staff file.

**Good to know:**

- Signing is logged with a timestamp — it is your acknowledgement on record.

---

### My Payslips

Your own payslips from locked payroll months — view and download your pay record. Read-only.

**Who can use this:** Every employee, for themselves.

**What you can do here:**

- **View / download a payslip** — Opens your payslip for a locked month.

**Good to know:**

- Payslips appear once a month's payroll is locked; figures mirror the control sheet, not necessarily the bank payment (which runs in Remita).

---

### Change Password

Change your own sign-in password. Reached from the top bar.

**Who can use this:** Every signed-in user, for themselves.

**What you can do here:**

- **Change password** — Sets a new password for your account.

**Good to know:**

- If you're still on the shared initial password, change it here now.

---

### My Profile

Your own account details — the personal information you can maintain about yourself. Reached from the top bar, not the sidebar.

**Who can use this:** Every signed-in user, for themselves.

**What you can do here:**

- **Update my profile** — Saves changes to your own details. The portal always acts on you, the signed-in user.

**Good to know:**

- Some fields are maintained by People Ops on the employee record, not here.
